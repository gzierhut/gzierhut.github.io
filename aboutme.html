About Me

My name is Gwen. I am autistic and have a special interest in physics. In particular, I find quantum physics interesting. Also, I like cats and octopuses. I can be kind of weird and crazy.

I have a flame-point Siamese cat named Casper. He is very affectionate. He has done a lot of funny things. One time, he rolled in a chalk drawing and got pink spots all over himself. Another time, he stole one of my mother's slippers and hit it on my bedroom door.
